Dream diary, 2014

20 days & 20 nights

Over the course of 20 days and 20 nights I have documented my dreams and my daily experiences. In this experiment the conscious is juxtaposed to the subconscious in the form of a dice-game. Every roll of the dice affects this chance-generated juxtaposition of reality and dream.
